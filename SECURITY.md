## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security vulnerabilities seriously. If you discover a security issue, please do NOT open a public issue. Instead, please send an email to **jiyisoon@163.com**.

1.  Include "Security Vulnerability" in the subject line.
2.  Describe the vulnerability in detail.
3.  Provide steps to reproduce the issue.
4.  We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours.
5.  We will keep you updated on our progress as we resolve the issue.

Thank you for helping keep SoonFx Engine safe!
